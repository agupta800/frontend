Test Requirements:

The entrance examination study in the USA for Nepalese students depends on the course choice. English language proficiency tests that are common for all courses are IELTS, PTE and TOEFL. It is mandatory to qualify for either one of these exams or a specific one as demanded by the university to be eligible for the course along with the other entrance exam. SAT is the common entrance examination that is accepted for different courses in various colleges in America. The law entrance exam LSAT is mandatory for admission into law colleges in America.

Scholarships to Study in USA

The plan of study in the USA for Nepalese students can be a bit expensive, especially if they opt for private universities. But there are many scholarships provided to make your dream of studying in the USA come true. These scholarships provide a huge relief but are highly competitive, considering the large number of applicants. The applicants with better grades or with an inclination towards music or arts have a higher chance of receiving the scholarship.

The international students are provided with the following scholarships:

Types of USA Student Visas:

F-1 Visa J-1 Visa M-1 Visa
The F1-Visas are issued to students who are willing to pursue an educational program or study the English language at a recognized US college or university. All undergraduate and graduate programs come under the F1 Visa. The F1 status also allows part-time and on-campus employment for students The J1 Visa is extended to people who are willing to receive practical training and study any vocational courses in the USA. These visas are also for visiting scholars and working professionals who are pursuing an exchange program. The M1 Visas are issued to students who take up a vocational program or non-academic course in the US. Students under these visas are not allowed to work during the course of study.
